Care Instructions for Your Silk Saree

  • Dry clean only - recommended for the first time
  • If hand washing, use cold water and mild detergent
  • Do not soak for extended periods
  • Avoid direct sunlight when drying
  • Iron on low heat with a cloth in between
  • Store in a cotton cloth, away from moisture
  • Use naphthalene balls to prevent insect damage
  • Refold your saree periodically to avoid permanent creases
